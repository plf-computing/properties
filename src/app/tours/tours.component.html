Tours & Excursions

Discover the wonders of Watamu with our guided tours and excursions. Explore the Marine National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and snorkel or dive among the vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life.

Visit the ancient Gede Ruins, a 12th-century Swahili town, and learn about the rich history and culture of the region. Take a boat trip to the nearby islands, go deep-sea fishing, or simply relax on the pristine beaches of Watamu.
